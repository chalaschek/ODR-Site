Powering the Green Web

Open Data Registry (ODR) brings together Brands, Suppliers, & Developers around a common platform for securely sharing & processing supply chain data.

Built upon linked open data standards, ODR allows Brands and Suppliers to free their data and make it actionable via a secure data registry and access protocol.
